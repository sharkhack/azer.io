Tips & Tricks

  • You can power up by various soccer ball options

  • Ability of choosing color of the stadium and jersey of the opponent team

  • You can shoot faster with Fireball and increase your score

  • Magnetball sticks your finger: You can control Magnetball for 30 sec. as sliding your finger.

  • Use soccer cleats for self-defense: You can strut four opponent players by using soccer cleat.
